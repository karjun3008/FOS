Our goal is to inspire the students who are devoted to science for the welfare of society.

"Knowledge removes the darkness of ignorance in life."

Innovation happens when people from different background come together, think together and act together, challenge each other & help each other. NO ONE IS STRONGER THAN ALL OF US. In this modern era, no discipline can work in complete isolation. The absolute need of hour is to "generate" and "harness" multidisciplinary expertise. The peculiarity of multidisciplinary expertise is that it can not be learnt from books, internet and newspaper!!! It can only be acquired by actually being with people from different discipline, under one 'UMBRELLA'. And, this is what Fraternity of Seekers is all about!
